# 🏥 Saint Mary Hospital Data Insights (2019–2024)

**Tool:** Power BI | **Dataset:** 55,000 Patients | **Period:** 2019–2024 | **Domain:** Healthcare Analytics

---

## 📌 Project Overview

A comprehensive Power BI dashboard analyzing 6 years of hospital data from Saint Mary Hospital. The project uncovers patient demographics, medical condition trends, billing performance, and insurance dependency across 55,000 patients and $1.4 billion in revenue.

## 🔍 Key Findings

### 🩺 Medical Conditions
- **Arthritis** affects 9,218 patients and is most common among young adults
- **Asthma** is more prevalent in older adults
- **Diabetes** is more common in older adult females
- **Cancer** is more prevalent in older adult males

### 🏨 Admissions & Medications
- **Most common admission type:** Elective — indicating planned treatments over emergency visits
- **Most prescribed medication:** Lipitor
- **Condition with most abnormal test results:** Arthritis

### 💰 Billing & Insurance
- **Top insurance provider:** Cigna — covers 11,139 patients
- **Highest total payments:** Cigna at $284 million
- Insurance coverage is heavily skewed toward Cigna across all metrics

### 👥 Patient Profiles & Patterns
- **Most common profile:** Male, older adult
- **Female older adults** trend toward diabetes
- **Male older adults** trend toward cancer

---

## 💡 Key Insights

1. Arthritis is common in young adults but shows the **highest number of abnormal test results** — requiring early intervention strategies
2. **Older adults dominate** the patient population with chronic conditions like asthma, diabetes, and cancer
3. **Elective admissions dominate** — suggesting strong planned care culture over reactive emergency visits
4. **Insurance dependency is concentrated** in Cigna — a risk factor for hospital revenue planning

---

## 💼 Business Recommendations

1. Develop **targeted chronic disease programs** for older adults — especially diabetes and cancer
2. Investigate **Arthritis abnormal test results** — high frequency suggests need for improved diagnostic protocols
3. **Diversify insurance partnerships** beyond Cigna to reduce revenue concentration risk
4. Focus preventive care resources on **young adults with arthritis** before conditions worsen
